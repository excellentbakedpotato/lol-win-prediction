Can Early Game Stats Predict Who Wins in League of Legends?

A data science project analyzing professional League of Legends esports matches

Introduction

This project investigates whether early game statistics — specifically gold, XP, and CS differences at 15 minutes — can predict the outcome of professional League of Legends matches. We use data from Oracle's Elixir, covering thousands of professional games across major leagues worldwide.

Research Question

Can you tell who's going to win a League of Legends game just from the first 15 minutes?
